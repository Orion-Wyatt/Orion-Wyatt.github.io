Unit 6 - Creating Digital Graphics

Unit 6: Creating Digital Graphics focuses on the features and uses of digital graphics. This is off the fact that digital graphics are found present all over the internet, as well as around us when we're out and about.

This unit is assessed through assignment work and consists of 3 assignment.

Assignment 1 - Researching Digital Graphics

Unit 6: Assignment 1 asks for you to investigate two digital graphics. One being a Bitmap Graphic (A graphic made up of pixels), and the other being a Vector Graphic (A graphic made up of lines drawn from coordinates).
