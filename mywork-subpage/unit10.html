Unit 10 - Database Development

Unit 10: Database Development looks towards the creation and management of databases. This is focusing on a more simple database rather than the complex database found on most sites, such as Amazon or Google for example.

This unit is assessed through assignment work. There are 3 assignments in this unit.

Assignment 1 - The Uses And Features Of Databases

This assignment is a research assignment that asks for the user to choose 2 different databases to research. This is done as a presentation (plus speaker's notes) and also stands to assist later on into the unit as it requires you to learn about parts of a database.
